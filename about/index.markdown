<p>I’ve been a developer, project manager, scrum master and product marketing manager. I believe in web services — CRM, SaaS, analytics software, advertising platforms, and any comprehensive back-end system with a simple frontend user experience. I'm a creator of <a href="http://kudobox.co" rel="nofollow">Kudobox</a> and the main contributor to <a href="https://github.com/HappyMelly/teller" rel="nofollow">Happy Melly Teller</a>.</p>
<p>I think good technical products actually make life easier, better and cheaper. Creating products is awesome because it enables you to create relationships with two groups people — your customers and your team. Improving relationships with one of these group automatically helps you to improve relationships with another one.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>

<pre>I’m going on a world tour to learn what fantastic, transparent and innovative companies are doing. From April 1 to June 26, I’ll be traveling around sunny and warm — for a Russian! — Finland, Sweden, Northern Germany (Berlin and Hamburg), the Netherlands, Barcelona / greater Catalunya / Catalonia, and perhaps France. Then, later in the 2015, I’ll head to the States and Canada.</pre>
