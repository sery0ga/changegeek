Why should you invite me?

Exchange experience

I have experience in many spheres, from leading teams to running local Agile community to developing international network of businesses.

I happily take part in meetups, coding sessions and any other activities which help us to exchange knowledge and know each other better.

Blog about you

Inspiring companies which care about employees and their well-being have been a topic of my interest for several years now. I like to find great organisations and write about them (check Futurice case study as an example).

I gladly share my findings about your company on my own and Happy Melly blogs.

Inspire others

Going on a tour and visiting terrific companies around the world is one of my dreams. And I'm doing it while keep working and helping others be happier at work.

I hope my small trip will motivate other people to pursue much bigger dreams. Help me to inspire people!

Recommendations

Peter Hilton
Software developer,
writer, speaker,
ex-COO at Lunatech
When Sergey visited our office for two weeks to exchange ideas and meet new people, I was impressed by how much he was able to bring to our team. Sergey is not just an excellent developer, but offered valuable insights in his role as an agile coach, and infected the team with his enthusiasm and positive outlook. I recommend that anyone who has the opportunity should spend a few days with Sergey and revitalise their work.

Why am I doing it?

Short answer
Because I can
Long answer
I've been a big fan and active promoter of organizations which care about happiness and engagement of their employees for several years. I learned a lot by reading books and articles, watching interviews and talks. Now I want to visit awesome companies, talk to people who work there, see how they produce great products.
